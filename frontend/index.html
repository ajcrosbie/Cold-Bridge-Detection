Before you upload

  • Stand roughly 2m away from the wall when taking the thermal photo.
  • Keep the camera as straight-on to the wall as possible.
  • Try to include the full wall section you want analysed.
  • Avoid blurry shots, reflective surfaces, and mixed indoor/outdoor framing.
  • You can upload multiple photos at once, or a ZIP file containing multiple thermal images.
